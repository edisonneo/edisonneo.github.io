You wake up in a room.

>

Rules

  1. To move around rooms type in ‘go ____’. You can move in the north, south, east and west directions.(e.g ‘go east’)
  2. If you are in doubt where you are, type in ‘look’.
  3. Type in ‘search around’ to discover details of the room and find important items.
  4. Type in ‘use’ , ‘throw’ , ‘take’ with the item that you want to interact with. (e.g. ‘take pen’, ‘use paper’)
  5. Type ‘inventory’ to check what you have in your bag.
  6. Remember to search and explore all directions in order to get the items you need.
